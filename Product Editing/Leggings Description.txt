Q&A

Can your leggings be worn for working out?

Absolutely! Our leggings are designed to be stretchy and flexible enough for all activities, including workouts. Check out our selections of athletic leggings and capris for additional benefits during your workout.

What materials are your products made of?

Most of our products are made with a polyester-elastane blend, with the exception of our cotton and faux-leather leggings.

Are they machine washable?

Yes! Like all our products, we recommend a cold wash and low tumble dry to keep your leggings in their best condition.

Are your leggings see-through?

Our leggings are designed with each our customers in mind. No one wants to wear leggings and have to worry about their under garments being visible, therefore we ensure that each of our leggings, and capris are all tested before leaving our facilities.

Is the waistband on your leggings tight fit comfortably around the waist?

Yes! The waistband on our leggings is designed to allow at least 1 in of stretch around the waist so that it provides maximum comfort and functionality

Will all of my products now have the Lotus Leggings logo?

Yes, all products will now be manufactured with the Lotus Leggings logo on them!

Will the colors in my leggings fade once I wash them?

Absolutely not! Our leggings are each printed using high quality sublimation inks that have been tested to withstand continuous washing of our leggings!

Is the material good for cold weather?

Our products are designed to maintain your natural body heat. Wearing our products combined with your favorite winter gear will definitely keep you warm this winter!

Are all of your products shipped with the Lotus Leggings logo on them?

Yes! We are now including the Lotus logo on all of our products!

How durable are the seams of your products?

Our products are all sewn together using the 7-point stitch method to ensure that our products are built with maximum strength and quality.
